Investigation of bike hiring trips¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to investigate trip characteristics and take a look at the features of user, trip and time factor

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset consists of (183412, 20) columns and rows respectively.
It describes bike trips in Feb 2019 with some data about users including their birth year, user type and gender and some features abou the trip icluding duration and stations.

Distribution of Diamond Prices¶

Diamond prices in the dataset take on a very large range of values, from about \$300 at the lowest, to about \$19,000 at the highest. Plotted on a logarithmic scale, the distribution of diamond prices takes on a multimodal shape.
